Work Process

My Work Process

Discovery

Read more

During this stage, discussions with the client occur regarding their needs and expectations for the future website. This may include functional requirements, design, target audience, timelines, and budget. The outcome of this stage should be a clear technical specification or brief that will serve as the foundation for the entire development process.

Design

Read more

Creation of the website's design and functionality concept based on the gathered requirements. This involves finding references, choosing a color scheme, fonts, and overall user interface. Maintaining open communication with the client allows for adjustments to the concept according to their feedback.

Build

Read more

Direct development of the website using selected technologies and tools. This includes coding, graphic design, content integration, and functionality testing. Incremental updates to the client about the progress and providing opportunities for feedback are crucial during this stage.

Completion

Read more

Conducting final testing, fixing bugs, and optimizing performance. Approval of the final version by the client and the launch of the website: hosting it and registering the domain name. The client is provided with the necessary documentation and training on managing and maintaining the website, if specified in the contract.
